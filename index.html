Our Mission

Hangar 33 Brew Co. was born from a deep respect for service and a lifelong love of damn good coffee. Founded by a former Air Force Colonel, our mission is to unite the service industry, whether military or civilian, around a brew that honors the call, regardless of the uniform. We don’t just roast beans, we salute effort. We support those who serve, and we do it with integrity, service before self, and excellence in all we do.

Our Vision

Become the premier coffee brand for veterans, active duty, first responders, and their supporters - built on camaraderie, purpose, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Why Choose Us?

Hangar 33 isn’t just coffee, it’s a code. Every roast we craft is a tribute to those who wake up early, push through hard days, and answer the call - whether it's in combat boots, turnout gear, duty belt, or scrubs.

Giving Back

For every 20 bags sold this year, we’ll donate one bag to the men and women who serve — firefighters, police, healthcare workers, and military teams. Come December, we’ll deliver those bags to keep their spirits strong. Our 2026 goal: Donate 100 bags. Help us hit the mark!
